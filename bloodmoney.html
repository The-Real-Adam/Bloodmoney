Whistleblower: Bloodmoney

In the tool kit of “Fake News” hangs the tactic of “No News.” — what those in power use to negate the stories and platforms of the people who expose their lies. Whistleblower Blood Money is a real life thriller following banking insider John Cruz’s daring campaign to expose one of the largest money laundering schemes in U.S. banking history at HSBC. The book chronicles his fight to expose the truth, and details the curious silence he encountered as his efforts were nearly thwarted at every turn. But he would not remain silent. Whistleblower Blood Money reveals the anatomy of a cover-up, lived from the inside by an honest man—an employee of HSBC, who would not look the other way and pressed his evidence until the powers that be had to listen.
